PSALM 135

vv. 123456789101112131415161718192021

Ps 135:1Hallelujah! Praise the name of Jehovah; / Praise Him, you servants of Jehovah,

Ps 135:2Who stand in the house of Jehovah, / In the courts of the house of our God.

Ps 135:3Praise Jehovah, for Jehovah is good; / Sing psalms to His name, for it is pleasant.

Ps 135:4For Jehovah has chosen Jacob for Himself, / Israel for His personal treasure.

Ps 135:5For I know that Jehovah is great / And that our Lord is above all gods.

Ps 135:6Whatever Jehovah pleases He does / In heaven and on earth, / In the seas and all the depths.

Ps 135:7He causes the vapors to ascend from the ends of the earth; / He makes lightning for the rain; / He brings forth wind from His treasuries.

Ps 135:8He struck the firstborn of Egypt, / Both of man and of beast.

Ps 135:9He sent signs and wonders into your midst, O Egypt, / Upon Pharaoh and all his servants.

Ps 135:10He struck many nations / And slew mighty kings:

Ps 135:11Sihon the king of the Amorites / And Og the king of Bashan / And all the kingdoms of Canaan.

Ps 135:12And He gave their land as an inheritance, / An inheritance to Israel His people.

Ps 135:13O Jehovah, Your name is forever; / O Jehovah, Your memorial is from generation to generation.

Ps 135:14For Jehovah will execute judgment for His people, / And He will have compassion concerning His servants.

Ps 135:15The idols of the nations are mere silver and gold, / The work of human hands.

Ps 135:16They have mouths, but they do not speak; / They have eyes, but they do not see.

Ps 135:17They have ears, but they do not give ear; / And there is no breath in their mouths.

Ps 135:18Those who make them, / All who trust in them, / Will be like them.

Ps 135:19O house of Israel, bless Jehovah. / O house of Aaron, bless Jehovah.

Ps 135:20O house of Levi, bless Jehovah. / O you who fear Jehovah, bless Jehovah.

Ps 135:21Blessed be Jehovah from Zion, / Who dwells in Jerusalem. / Hallelujah.
