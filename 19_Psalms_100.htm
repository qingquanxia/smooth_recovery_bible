PSALM 100

vv. 12345

A Psalm of thanksgiving

Ps 100:1Make a joyful noise to Jehovah, all the earth.

Ps 100:2Serve Jehovah with rejoicing; / Come before His presence with joyful singing.

Ps 100:3Know that it is Jehovah who is God; / It is He who has made us and not we ourselves. / We are His people and the sheep of His pasture.

Ps 100:4Enter His gates with thanksgiving, / His courts with praise; / Give thanks to Him; bless His name.

Ps 100:5For Jehovah is good; His lovingkindness is forever, / And His faithfulness is to all generations.
