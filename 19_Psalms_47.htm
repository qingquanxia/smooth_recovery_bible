To the choir director. Of the sons of Korah. A Psalm

Ps 47:1Clap your hands, all you peoples; / Shout to God with the voice of a triumphant sound.

Ps 47:2For Jehovah Most High is awesome: / A great King over all the earth.

Ps 47:3He subdues peoples under us, / And nations under our feet.

Ps 47:4He chose our inheritance for us, / The excellency of Jacob, whom He loved. Selah

Ps 47:5God ascends amidst shouting; / Jehovah, at the sound of a trumpet.

Ps 47:6Sing psalms to God, sing psalms; / Sing psalms to our King, sing psalms.

Ps 47:7For God is the King of all the earth; / Sing psalms with understanding.

Ps 47:8God reigns over the nations; / God sits upon His holy throne.

Ps 47:9The princes of the peoples have been gathered / As the people of the God of Abraham; / For the shields of the earth belong to God; / He is greatly exalted.
