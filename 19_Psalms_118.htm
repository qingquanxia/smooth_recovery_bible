PSALM 118

vv. 1234567891011121314151617181920212223242526272829

Ps 118:1Give thanks to Jehovah, for He is good, / For His lovingkindness is forever.

Ps 118:2Let Israel say, / His lovingkindness is forever.

Ps 118:3Let the house of Aaron say, / His lovingkindness is forever.

Ps 118:4Let those who fear Jehovah say, / His lovingkindness is forever.

Ps 118:5Out of my distress I called upon Jehovah; / Jehovah answered me and set me in a place broad and free.

Ps 118:6Jehovah is for me; I will not be afraid. / What can man do to me?

Ps 118:7Jehovah is for me as my Helper, / And I will see what should happen to those who hate me.

Ps 118:8It is better to take refuge in Jehovah / Than to trust in man.

Ps 118:9It is better to take refuge in Jehovah / Than to trust in princes.

Ps 118:10All nations surrounded me; / In the name of Jehovah I indeed cut them down!

Ps 118:11They surrounded me; yes, they surrounded me; / In the name of Jehovah I indeed cut them down!

Ps 118:12They surrounded me like bees; / They were extinguished like a fire of thorns; / In the name of Jehovah I indeed cut them down!

Ps 118:13I was pushed hard that I might fall; / But Jehovah helped me.

Ps 118:14Jehovah is my strength and my song, / And He has become my salvation.

Ps 118:15The voice of rejoicing and salvation / Is in the tents of the righteous. / The right hand of Jehovah does valiantly;

Ps 118:16The right hand of Jehovah is exalted; / The right hand of Jehovah does valiantly.

Ps 118:17I will not die, but live, / And declare the works of Jehovah.

Ps 118:18Jehovah has severely chastened me, / But He has not given me over to death.

Ps 118:19Open to me the gates of righteousness: / I will enter in through them; I will give thanks to Jehovah.

Ps 118:20This is the gate of Jehovah; / The righteous shall enter in through it.

Ps 118:21I will give thanks to You, for You have answered me / And have become my salvation.

Ps 118:22The stone which the builders rejected / Has become the head of the corner.

Ps 118:23This is from Jehovah; / It is wonderful in our sight.

Ps 118:24This is the day that Jehovah has made; / Let us exult and rejoice in it.

Ps 118:25O Jehovah, do save, we pray! / O Jehovah, do send prosperity, we pray!

Ps 118:26Blessed is He who comes in the name of Jehovah; / We bless you from the house of Jehovah.

Ps 118:27Jehovah is God, / And He has given us light; / Bind the sacrifice of the feast with cords / To the horns of the altar.

Ps 118:28You are my God, and I give thanks to You. / You are my God; I will exalt You.

Ps 118:29Give thanks to Jehovah, for He is good, / For His lovingkindness is forever.
