PSALM 121

vv. 12345678

A Song of Ascents

Ps 121:1I will lift up my eyes to the hills. / From where will my help come?

Ps 121:2My help comes from Jehovah, / Who made heaven and earth.

Ps 121:3He will not let your foot slip; / He who keeps you will not slumber.

Ps 121:4Behold, Israel’s Keeper / Will neither slumber nor sleep.

Ps 121:5Jehovah is your Keeper; / Jehovah is your shade at your right hand.

Ps 121:6The sun will not strike you by day, / Nor the moon by night.

Ps 121:7Jehovah will keep you from all evil; / He will keep your soul.

Ps 121:8Jehovah will keep your going out and your coming in / From now and to eternity.
