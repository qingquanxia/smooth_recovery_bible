PSALM 24

vv. 12345678910

A Psalm of David

Ps 24:1The earth is Jehovah’s, and its fullness, / The habitable land and those who dwell in it.

Ps 24:2For it is He who founded it upon the seas / And established it upon the streams.

Ps 24:3Who may ascend the mountain of Jehovah, / And who may stand in His holy place?

Ps 24:4He who has clean hands and a pure heart, / Who has not lifted up his soul to falsehood / Or sworn deceitfully.

Ps 24:5He will receive blessing from Jehovah, / And righteousness from the God of his salvation.

Ps 24:6This is the generation of those who seek Him, / Those who seek Your face, even Jacob. Selah

Ps 24:7Lift up your heads, O gates; / And be lifted up, O long enduring doors; / And the King of glory will come in.

Ps 24:8Who is the King of glory? / Jehovah strong and mighty! / Jehovah mighty in battle!

Ps 24:9Lift up your heads, O gates; / And lift up, O long enduring doors; / And the King of glory will come in.

Ps 24:10Who is this King of glory? / Jehovah of hosts — / He is the King of glory! Selah
