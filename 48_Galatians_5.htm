GALATIANS 5

vv. 1234567891011121314151617181920212223242526

Ga 5:1It is for freedom that Christ has set us free; stand fast therefore, and do not be entangled with a yoke of slavery again.

Ga 5:2Behold, I Paul say to you that if you become circumcised, Christ will profit you nothing.

Ga 5:3And I testify again to every man who becomes circumcised that he is a debtor to do the whole law.

Ga 5:4You have been brought to nought, separated from Christ, you who are being justified by law; you have fallen from grace.

Ga 5:5For we by the Spirit out of faith eagerly await the hope of righteousness.

Ga 5:6For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision avails anything nor uncircumcision, but faith avails, operating through love.

Ga 5:7You were running well. Who hindered you that you would not believe and obey the truth?

Ga 5:8This persuasion is not of Him who calls you.

Ga 5:9A little leaven leavens the whole lump.

Ga 5:10I am confident in the Lord concerning you that you will be of no other mind; but he who is troubling you shall bear the judgment, whoever he may be.

Ga 5:11But I, brothers, if I still preach circumcision, why am I still being persecuted? Then the stumbling block of the cross has been annulled.

Ga 5:12I wish that those upsetting you would even cut themselves off.

Ga 5:13For you were called for freedom, brothers; only do not turn this freedom into an opportunity for the flesh, but through love serve one another.

Ga 5:14For the whole law is fulfilled in one word, in this, “You shall love your neighbor as yourself.”

Ga 5:15But if you bite and devour one another, beware lest you be consumed by one another.

Ga 5:16But I say, Walk by the Spirit and you shall by no means fulfill the lust of the flesh.

Ga 5:17For the flesh lusts against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh; for these oppose each other that you would not do the things that you desire.

Ga 5:18But if you are led by the Spirit, you are not under the law.

Ga 5:19And the works of the flesh are manifest, which are such things as fornication, uncleanness, lasciviousness,

Ga 5:20idolatry, sorcery, enmities, strife, jealousy, outbursts of anger, factions, divisions, sects,

Ga 5:21envyings, bouts of drunkenness, carousings, and things like these, of which I tell you beforehand, even as I have said before, that those who practice such things will not inherit the kingdom of God.

Ga 5:22But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, long-suffering, kindness, goodness, faithfulness,

Ga 5:23meekness, self-control; against such things there is no law.

Ga 5:24But they who are of Christ Jesus have crucified the flesh with its passions and its lusts.

Ga 5:25If we live by the Spirit, let us also walk by the Spirit.

Ga 5:26Let us not become vainglorious, provoking one another, envying one another.
