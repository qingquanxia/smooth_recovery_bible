PSALM 116

vv. 12345678910111213141516171819

Ps 116:1I love Jehovah because He hears / My voice, my supplications,

Ps 116:2Because He inclines His ear to me; / Therefore I will call upon Him all my days.

Ps 116:3The bonds of death encompassed me, / And the distresses of Sheol fell upon me; / I fell upon trouble and sorrow.

Ps 116:4But I called upon the name of Jehovah. / O Jehovah, I pray, deliver my soul.

Ps 116:5Jehovah is gracious and righteous, / Indeed our God is compassionate.

Ps 116:6Jehovah preserves the simple: / I was brought low, and He saved me.

Ps 116:7Return to your rest, O my soul; / For Jehovah has dealt bountifully with you.

Ps 116:8For You have delivered my soul from death, / My eyes from tears, / My feet from stumbling.

Ps 116:9I will walk before Jehovah / In the land of the living.

Ps 116:10I believed, therefore I spoke; / I was afflicted greatly.

Ps 116:11I said in my haste, / All men are liars.

Ps 116:12What shall I return to Jehovah / For all His benefits toward me?

Ps 116:13I will take up the cup of salvation / And call upon the name of Jehovah.

Ps 116:14I will pay my vows to Jehovah, / Oh, in the presence of all His people.

Ps 116:15Precious in the sight of Jehovah / Is the death of His faithful ones.

Ps 116:16O Jehovah! / Surely I am Your servant; / I am Your servant, the son of Your female servant; / You have loosed my bonds.

Ps 116:17To You I will offer a sacrifice of thanksgiving, / And I will call upon the name of Jehovah.

Ps 116:18I will pay my vows to Jehovah, / Oh, in the presence of all His people,

Ps 116:19In the courts of the house of Jehovah, / In the midst of you, O Jerusalem. / Hallelujah.
