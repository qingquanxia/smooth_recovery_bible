2 CORINTHIANS 2

vv. 1234567891011121314151617

2Co 2:1But I determined this for myself, that I would not come again to you in sorrow.

2Co 2:2For if I cause you sorrow, who then is the one who makes me glad, except the one who is made sorrowful by me?

2Co 2:3And I wrote this very thing to you that when I come I would not have sorrow from those who ought to make me rejoice, having confidence in you all that my joy is the joy of you all.

2Co 2:4For out of much affliction and anguish of heart I wrote to you through many tears, not that you would be made sorrowful but that you would know the love which I have more abundantly toward you.

2Co 2:5But if anyone has caused sorrow, he has not caused me to sorrow, but in part (lest I lay too heavy a burden) all of you.

2Co 2:6Sufficient for such a one is this punishment by the majority,

2Co 2:7so that on the contrary you should rather forgive and comfort him, lest perhaps such a one be swallowed up with excessive sorrow.

2Co 2:8Therefore I exhort you to confirm your love toward him.

2Co 2:9For to this end also I wrote, that I might know your approvedness, whether you are obedient in all things.

2Co 2:10But whom you forgive anything, I also forgive; for also what I have forgiven, if I have forgiven anything, it is for your sake in the person of Christ;

2Co 2:11that we may not be taken advantage of by Satan, for we are not ignorant of his schemes.

2Co 2:12Furthermore, when I came to Troas for the gospel of Christ and a door was open to me in the Lord,

2Co 2:13I had no rest in my spirit, for I did not find Titus my brother; but taking leave of them, I went forth into Macedonia.

2Co 2:14But thanks be to God, who always leads us in triumph in the Christ and manifests the savor of the knowledge of Him through us in every place.

2Co 2:15For we are a fragrance of Christ to God in those who are being saved and in those who are perishing:

2Co 2:16to some a savor out of death unto death, and to the others a savor out of life unto life. And who is sufficient for these things?

2Co 2:17For we are not like the many, adulterating the word of God for profit; but as out of sincerity, but as out of God, before God we speak in Christ.
