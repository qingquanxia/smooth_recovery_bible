EPHESIANS 3

vv. 123456789101112131415161718192021

Ep 3:1For this cause I Paul, the prisoner of Christ Jesus on behalf of you, the Gentiles —

Ep 3:2if indeed you have heard of the stewardship of the grace of God which was given to me for you,

Ep 3:3that by revelation the mystery was made known to me, as I have written previously in brief,

Ep 3:4by which, in reading it, you can perceive my understanding in the mystery of Christ,

Ep 3:5which in other generations was not made known to the sons of men, as it has now been revealed to His holy apostles and prophets in spirit,

Ep 3:6that in Christ Jesus the Gentiles are fellow heirs and fellow members of the Body and fellow partakers of the promise through the gospel,

Ep 3:7of which I became a minister according to the gift of the grace of God which was given to me according to the operation of His power.

Ep 3:8To me, less than the least of all saints, was this grace given to announce to the Gentiles the unsearchable riches of Christ as the gospel

Ep 3:9and to enlighten all that they may see what the economy of the mystery is, which throughout the ages has been hidden in God, who created all things,

Ep 3:10in order that now to the rulers and the authorities in the heavenlies the multifarious wisdom of God might be made known through the church,

Ep 3:11according to the eternal purpose which He made in Christ Jesus our Lord,

Ep 3:12in whom we have boldness and access in confidence through faith in Him.

Ep 3:13Therefore I ask you not to faint at my afflictions for your sake, since they are your glory.

Ep 3:14For this cause I bow my knees unto the Father,

Ep 3:15of whom every family in the heavens and on earth is named,

Ep 3:16that He would grant you, according to the riches of His glory, to be strengthened with power through His Spirit into the inner man,

Ep 3:17that Christ may make His home in your hearts through faith, that you, being rooted and grounded in love,

Ep 3:18may be full of strength to apprehend with all the saints what the breadth and length and height and depth are

Ep 3:19and to know the knowledge-surpassing love of Christ, that you may be filled unto all the fullness of God.

Ep 3:20But to Him who is able to do superabundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power which operates in us,

Ep 3:21to Him be the glory in the church and in Christ Jesus unto all the generations forever and ever. Amen.
