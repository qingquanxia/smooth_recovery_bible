JOB 25

vv. 123456

Jb 25:1Then Bildad the Shuhite answered and said,

Jb 25:2Dominion and dread are with Him; / He makes peace in His high places.

Jb 25:3Is there any number to His armies? / And upon whom does His light not arise?

Jb 25:4How then can a man be righteous with God? / And how can one born of a woman be pure?

Jb 25:5Indeed, even the moon has no brightness, / And the stars are not pure in His eyes.

Jb 25:6How much less a man, a maggot; / And the son of man, a worm!
