PSALM 120

vv. 1234567

A Song of Ascents

Ps 120:1In my distress I called out to Jehovah, / And He answered me.

Ps 120:2O Jehovah, deliver my soul from lying lips, / From a tongue of deceit.

Ps 120:3What should be given to you, / And what further shall be done to you, / O tongue of deceit?

Ps 120:4The sharp arrows of a mighty man, / With the burning coals of the broom tree!

Ps 120:5Woe is me, for I sojourn in Meshech, / I dwell among the tents of Kedar.

Ps 120:6Long has my soul had its dwelling / With him who hates peace.

Ps 120:7I am for peace; but when I speak, / They are for war.
