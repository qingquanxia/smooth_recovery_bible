A Psalm; a Song for the Dedication of the House. Of David

Ps 30:1I extol You, O Jehovah, for You have drawn me up / And have not let my enemies rejoice over me.

Ps 30:2O Jehovah my God, / I cried out to You, / And You healed me.

Ps 30:3O Jehovah, You have brought up my soul from Sheol; / You have preserved me alive that I would not go down into the pit.

Ps 30:4Sing psalms to Jehovah, you who are His faithful ones; / And praise His holy memorial.

Ps 30:5For His anger lasts but a moment, / His favor, a lifetime; / Weeping may linger in the evening, / But a ringing shout goes up in the morning.

Ps 30:6As for me, I said in my prosperity, / I will never be shaken.

Ps 30:7O Jehovah, by Your favor You had made my mountain stand strong. / You hid Your face; I was troubled.

Ps 30:8To You, O Jehovah, I called out; / And to Jehovah I made my supplication.

Ps 30:9What profit is there in my blood if I go down into the pit? / Will the dust praise You? Will it declare Your faithfulness?

Ps 30:10Hear, O Jehovah, and be gracious to me. / O Jehovah, be my help.

Ps 30:11You have turned for me my mourning into dancing; / You have loosened my sackcloth / And girded me with rejoicing,

Ps 30:12That my glory might sing psalms to You and not be silent. / O Jehovah my God, I will praise You forever.
