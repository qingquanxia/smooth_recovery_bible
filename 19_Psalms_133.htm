PSALM 133

vv. 123

A Song of Ascents. Of David

Ps 133:1Behold, how good and how pleasant it is / For brothers to dwell in unity!

Ps 133:2It is like the fine oil upon the head / That ran down upon the beard, / Upon Aaron’s beard, / That ran down upon the hem of his garments;

Ps 133:3Like the dew of Hermon / That came down upon the mountains of Zion. / For there Jehovah commanded the blessing: / Life forever.
