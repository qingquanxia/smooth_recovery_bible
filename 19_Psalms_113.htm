PSALM 113

vv. 123456789

Ps 113:1Hallelujah! / Praise, you servants of Jehovah, / Praise the name of Jehovah.

Ps 113:2Let the name of Jehovah be blessed, / From now and to eternity.

Ps 113:3From the rising of the sun to its setting / The name of Jehovah is to be praised.

Ps 113:4Jehovah is high above all the nations, / And His glory is above the heavens.

Ps 113:5Who is like Jehovah our God, / Who has set His throne on high,

Ps 113:6Who humbles Himself to behold / The heavens and the earth?

Ps 113:7He raises the poor from the dust; / From the ash heap He lifts the needy;

Ps 113:8That He may seat them with princes, / With the princes of His people.

Ps 113:9He causes the barren woman to keep house / As a joyful mother of children. / Hallelujah.
