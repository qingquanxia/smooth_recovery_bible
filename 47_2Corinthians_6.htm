2 CORINTHIANS 6

vv. 123456789101112131415161718

2Co 6:1And working together with Him, we also entreat you not to receive the grace of God in vain;

2Co 6:2for He says, “In an acceptable time I listened to you, and in the day of salvation I helped you.” Behold, now is the well-acceptable time; behold, now is the day of salvation.

2Co 6:3We give no occasion of stumbling in anything that the ministry may not be faulted,

2Co 6:4but in everything we commend ourselves as ministers of God, in much endurance, in afflictions, in necessities, in distresses,

2Co 6:5in stripes, in imprisonments, in tumults, in labors, in watchings, in fastings;

2Co 6:6in pureness, in knowledge, in long-suffering, in kindness, in a holy spirit, in unfeigned love,

2Co 6:7in the word of truth, in the power of God; through the weapons of righteousness on the right and on the left,

2Co 6:8through glory and dishonor, through evil report and good report; as deceivers and yet true;

2Co 6:9as unknown and yet well known; as dying and yet behold we live; as being disciplined and yet not being put to death;

2Co 6:10as made sorrowful yet always rejoicing; as poor yet enriching many; as having nothing and yet possessing all things.

2Co 6:11Our mouth is opened to you, Corinthians; our heart is enlarged.

2Co 6:12You are not constricted in us, but you are constricted in your inward parts.

2Co 6:13But for a recompense in kind, I speak as to children, you also be enlarged.

2Co 6:14Do not become dissimilarly yoked with unbelievers. For what partnership do righteousness and lawlessness have? Or what fellowship does light have with darkness?

2Co 6:15And what concord does Christ have with Belial? Or what part does a believer have with an unbeliever?

2Co 6:16And what agreement does the temple of God have with idols? For we are the temple of the living God, even as God said, “I will dwell among them and walk among them; and I will be their God, and they will be My people.”

2Co 6:17Therefore “come out from their midst and be separated, says the Lord, and do not touch what is unclean; and I will welcome you”;

2Co 6:18“and I will be a Father to you, and you will be sons and daughters to Me, says the Lord Almighty.”
