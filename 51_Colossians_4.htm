COLOSSIANS 4

vv. 123456789101112131415161718

Co 4:1Masters, grant to your slaves that which is just and equal, knowing that you also have a Master in heaven.

Co 4:2Persevere in prayer, watching in it with thanksgiving,

Co 4:3praying at the same time for us also, that God would open to us a door for the word, to speak the mystery of Christ (because of which also I am bound),

Co 4:4that I may make it manifest as I ought to speak.

Co 4:5Walk in wisdom toward those who are without, redeeming the time.

Co 4:6Let your speech be always with grace, seasoned with salt, that you may know how you ought to answer each one.

Co 4:7All the things concerning me, Tychicus, the beloved brother and faithful minister and fellow slave in the Lord, will make known to you,

Co 4:8whom I have sent to you for this very thing, that you might know the things concerning us and that he might comfort your hearts;

Co 4:9with Onesimus, the faithful and beloved brother, who is one of you. They will make known to you all the things here.

Co 4:10Aristarchus, my fellow prisoner, greets you, as well as Mark, the cousin of Barnabas (concerning whom you received commandments, If he comes to you, receive him),

Co 4:11and Jesus, who is called Justus; these alone of the circumcision are my fellow workers for the kingdom of God, ones who have become a consolation to me.

Co 4:12Epaphras, who is one of you, a slave of Christ Jesus, greets you, always struggling on your behalf in his prayers that you may stand mature and fully assured in all the will of God.

Co 4:13For I testify of him that he labors much for you and for those in Laodicea and for those in Hierapolis.

Co 4:14Luke, the beloved physician, greets you, as well as Demas.

Co 4:15Greet the brothers in Laodicea, as well as Nymphas and the church, which is in his house.

Co 4:16And when this letter is read among you, cause that it be read in the church of the Laodiceans also, and that you also read the one from Laodicea.

Co 4:17And say to Archippus, Take heed to the ministry which you have received in the Lord, that you fulfill it.

Co 4:18The greeting in my own hand — Paul. Remember my bonds. Grace be with you.
