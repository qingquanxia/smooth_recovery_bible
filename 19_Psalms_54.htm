PSALM 54

vv. 1234567

To the choir director: on the stringed instruments. A Maschil of David, when the Ziphites came and said to Saul, David is indeed hiding among us!

Ps 54:1O God, save me by Your name, / And execute judgment for me by Your might.

Ps 54:2O God, hear my prayer; / Give ear to the words of my mouth.

Ps 54:3For strangers have risen up against me, / And those who terrorize seek my life; / They do not set God before them. Selah

Ps 54:4Behold, God is my Helper; / The Lord is among those who sustain my soul.

Ps 54:5He will return the evil to those who lie in wait for me. / Annihilate them in Your faithfulness.

Ps 54:6I will offer a freewill offering to You; / I will praise Your name, O Jehovah, for it is good.

Ps 54:7For He has delivered me from all distress, / And my eye looks triumphantly upon my enemies.
