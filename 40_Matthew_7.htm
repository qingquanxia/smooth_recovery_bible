MATTHEW 7

Mt 7:1Do not judge, that you be not judged.

Mt 7:2For with what judgment you judge, you shall be judged; and with what measure you measure, it shall be measured to you.

Mt 7:3And why do you look at the splinter which is in your brother’s eye, but the beam in your eye you do not consider?

Mt 7:4Or how can you say to your brother, Let me remove the splinter from your eye, and behold, the beam is in your eye?

Mt 7:5Hypocrite, first remove the beam from your eye, and then you will see clearly to remove the splinter from your brother’s eye.

Mt 7:6Do not give that which is holy to the dogs, neither cast your pearls before the hogs, lest they trample them with their feet, and turn and tear you.

Mt 7:7Ask and it shall be given to you; seek and you shall find; knock and it shall be opened to you.

Mt 7:8For everyone who asks receives, and he who seeks finds, and to him who knocks it shall be opened.

Mt 7:9Or what man is there among you who, when his son asks him for a loaf, will give him a stone?

Mt 7:10Or also when he asks for a fish, will give him a serpent?

Mt 7:11If you then being evil know how to give good gifts to your children, how much more will your Father who is in the heavens give good things to those who ask Him!

Mt 7:12Therefore all that you wish men would do to you, so also you do to them; for this is the law and the prophets.

Mt 7:13Enter in through the narrow gate, for wide is the gate and broad is the way that leads to destruction, and many are those who enter through it.

Mt 7:14Because narrow is the gate and constricted is the way that leads to life, and few are those who find it.

Mt 7:15Beware of false prophets, who come to you in sheep’s clothing, but inwardly they are ravenous wolves.

Mt 7:16By their fruits you will recognize them. Do men gather grapes from thorns, or figs from thistles?

Mt 7:17Even so every good tree produces good fruit, but the corrupt tree produces bad fruit.

Mt 7:18A good tree cannot produce bad fruit, neither can a corrupt tree produce good fruit.

Mt 7:19Every tree that does not produce good fruit is cut down and cast into the fire.

Mt 7:20So then, by their fruits you will recognize them.

Mt 7:21Not everyone who says to Me, Lord, Lord, will enter into the kingdom of the heavens, but he who does the will of My Father who is in the heavens.

Mt 7:22Many will say to Me in that day, Lord, Lord, was it not in Your name that we prophesied, and in Your name cast out demons, and in Your name did many works of power?

Mt 7:23And then I will declare to them: I never knew you. Depart from Me, you workers of lawlessness.

Mt 7:24Everyone therefore who hears these words of Mine and does them shall be likened to a prudent man who built his house upon the rock.

Mt 7:25And the rain descended, and the rivers came, and the winds blew, and they beat against that house; and it did not fall, for it was founded on the rock.

Mt 7:26And everyone who hears these words of Mine and does not do them shall be likened to a foolish man who built his house upon the sand.

Mt 7:27And the rain descended, and the rivers came, and the winds blew, and they dashed against that house; and it fell, and its fall was great.

Mt 7:28And when Jesus finished these words, the crowds were astounded at His teaching,

Mt 7:29for He taught them as One having authority and not like their scribes.
