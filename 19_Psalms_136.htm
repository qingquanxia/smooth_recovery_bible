PSALM 136

vv. 1234567891011121314151617181920212223242526

Ps 136:1Give thanks to Jehovah, for He is good; / For His lovingkindness is forever.

Ps 136:2Give thanks to the God of gods, / For His lovingkindness is forever.

Ps 136:3Give thanks to the Lord of lords, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:4To Him who alone does great wonders, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:5To Him who by understanding made the heavens, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:6To Him who spread forth the earth over the waters, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:7To Him who made the great lights, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:8The sun for ruling the day, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:9The moon and the stars for ruling the night, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:10To Him who struck Egypt in their firstborn, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:11And brought forth Israel from their midst, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:12With a mighty hand and an outstretched arm, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:13To Him who divided the Red Sea apart, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:14And brought Israel over through the midst of it, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:15And shook off Pharaoh and his force into the Red Sea, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:16To Him who marched His people through the wilderness, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:17To Him who struck great kings, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:18And slew majestic kings, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:19Sihon the king of the Amorites, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:20And Og the king of Bashan, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:21And gave their land as an inheritance, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:22An inheritance to Israel His servant, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:23Who remembered us in our low estate, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:24And rescued us from our adversaries, / For His lovingkindness is forever;

Ps 136:25Who gives food to all flesh, / For His lovingkindness is forever.

Ps 136:26Give thanks to the God of heaven, / For His lovingkindness is forever.
