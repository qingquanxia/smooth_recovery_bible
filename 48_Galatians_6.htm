GALATIANS 6

vv. 123456789101112131415161718

Ga 6:1Brothers, even if a man is overtaken in some offense, you who are spiritual restore such a one in a spirit of meekness, looking to yourself lest you also be tempted.

Ga 6:2Bear one another’s burdens, and in this way you will fulfill the law of Christ completely.

Ga 6:3For if anyone thinks he is something when he is nothing, he deceives himself.

Ga 6:4But let each man prove his own work, and then he will have his boast in regard to himself alone and not in regard to another.

Ga 6:5For each one shall bear his own load.

Ga 6:6But let him who is instructed in the word share in all good things with him who instructs.

Ga 6:7Do not be deceived: God is not mocked; for whatever a man sows, this he will also reap.

Ga 6:8For he who sows unto his own flesh will reap corruption of the flesh, but he who sows unto the Spirit will of the Spirit reap eternal life.

Ga 6:9And let us not lose heart in doing what is good, for in the proper season we will reap if we do not faint.

Ga 6:10So then, as we have the opportunity, let us do what is good toward all, but especially toward those of the household of the faith.

Ga 6:11See with what large letters I have written to you with my own hand.

Ga 6:12As many as desire to make a good show in the flesh, these compel you to be circumcised, but only that they may not be persecuted for the cross of Christ.

Ga 6:13For neither do they that become circumcised keep the law themselves, but they desire you to be circumcised that they may boast in your flesh.

Ga 6:14But far be it from me to boast except in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, through whom the world has been crucified to me and I to the world.

Ga 6:15For neither is circumcision anything nor uncircumcision, but a new creation is what matters.

Ga 6:16And as many as walk by this rule, peace be upon them and mercy, even upon the Israel of God.

Ga 6:17Henceforth let no one trouble me, for I bear in my body the brands of Jesus.

Ga 6:18The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit, brothers. Amen.
