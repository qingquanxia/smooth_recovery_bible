PSALM 95

vv. 1234567891011

Ps 95:1Come, let us sing for joy to Jehovah; / Let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation.

Ps 95:2Let us come before His presence with thanksgiving; / Let us make a joyful noise to Him with psalms.

Ps 95:3For Jehovah is a great God / And a great King above all gods.

Ps 95:4In His hand are the depths of the earth; / The heights of the mountains are His also.

Ps 95:5The sea is His, and He made it; / And His hands formed the dry land.

Ps 95:6Come, let us worship and bow down; / Let us kneel before Jehovah our Maker.

Ps 95:7For He is our God, / And we are the people of His pasture / And the flock of His hand. / Today, if you hear His voice,

Ps 95:8Do not harden your heart as at Meribah, / As in the day of Massah in the wilderness,

Ps 95:9When your fathers tested Me; / They tried Me, even though they had seen My work.

Ps 95:10For forty years I loathed that generation, / And I said, They are a people who go astray in heart; / And they do not know My ways;

Ps 95:11Therefore I swore in My anger: / They shall by no means enter into My rest!
