PSALM 134

vv. 123

A Song of Ascents

Ps 134:1Bless Jehovah now, / All you servants of Jehovah / Who stand by night in the house of Jehovah.

Ps 134:2Lift up your hands in the sanctuary, / And bless Jehovah.

Ps 134:3May Jehovah, who made heaven and earth, / Bless you from Zion.
