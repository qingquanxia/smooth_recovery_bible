PSALM 150

vv. 123456

Ps 150:1Hallelujah! / Praise God in His sanctuary; / Praise Him in the expanse that manifests His power.

Ps 150:2Praise Him for His mighty acts; / Praise Him according to His vast greatness.

Ps 150:3Praise Him with the blast of the trumpet; / Praise Him with the harp and lyre;

Ps 150:4Praise Him with the tambourine and with dancing; / Praise Him with the stringed instrument and the pipe.

Ps 150:5Praise Him with the loud cymbals; / Praise Him with the loud clanging cymbals.

Ps 150:6Let everything that has breath praise Jehovah. / Hallelujah!
