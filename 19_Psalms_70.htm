PSALM 70

vv. 12345

To the choir director. Of David, for remembrance

Ps 70:1O God, hasten to deliver me; / O Jehovah, hasten to help me.

Ps 70:2May those who seek my life / Be put to shame and confounded; / May those who take delight in my misfortune / Be turned back and humiliated;

Ps 70:3May those who say, Aha! Aha! / Turn away because of their shame;

Ps 70:4May all those who seek You / Be glad and rejoice in You; / And may those who love Your salvation / Say continually, / May God be magnified!

Ps 70:5But I am poor and needy; / O God, hasten to me. / You are my help and my Deliverer; / O Jehovah, do not delay.
