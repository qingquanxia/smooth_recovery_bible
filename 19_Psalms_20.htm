To the choir director. A Psalm of David

Ps 20:1May Jehovah answer you in the day of trouble; / May the name of the God of Jacob set you on high.

Ps 20:2May He send you help from the sanctuary / And support you from Zion.

Ps 20:3May He remember all your meal offerings / And accept your burnt offering. Selah

Ps 20:4May He give you according to your heart’s desire / And fulfill all your intention.

Ps 20:5May we shout victoriously in Your salvation / And raise the banner in the name of our God. / May Jehovah fulfill all your petitions.

Ps 20:6Now I know / That Jehovah saves His anointed; / He will answer him from His holy heaven / With mighty acts of salvation from His right hand.

Ps 20:7Some boast in chariots, and some in horses; / But we boast in the name of Jehovah our God.

Ps 20:8They are humbled and fallen, / But we have risen and stand upright.

Ps 20:9Save, O Jehovah! / May the king answer us when we call.
