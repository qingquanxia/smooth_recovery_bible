PSALM 87

vv. 1234567

Of the sons of Korah. A Psalm; a Song

Ps 87:1His foundation is in the holy mountains.

Ps 87:2Jehovah loves the gates of Zion / More than all the dwellings of Jacob.

Ps 87:3Glorious things are spoken of you, / O city of God. Selah

Ps 87:4I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon as those who know Me; / Behold, Philistia and Tyre along with Cush: / This one was born there, they say.

Ps 87:5But of Zion it will be said, / This one and that one were born in her, / And the Most High Himself will establish her.

Ps 87:6Jehovah will count / When He records the peoples: / This One was born there. Selah

Ps 87:7Then singing as well as dancing, they will say, / All my springs are in you.
